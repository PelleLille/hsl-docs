Halon Scripting Language
========================

Halon's scripting language is a domain-specific language (`DSL <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domain-specific_language>`_) for the `Halon MTA <http://halon.io/>`_, a platform for in- and outbound email. It's used to configure the behavior of email processing in a scriptable fashion.
